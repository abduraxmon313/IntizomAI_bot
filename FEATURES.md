- `/start`, `/help`, `/dashboard` buyruqlari
- Dashboard uchun WebApp button integratsiyasi
- Existing plan/reminder/report funksiyalari saqlangan

- Plans CRUD
- Goals CRUD
- Daily/weekly/monthly/yearly statistics
- Daily/weekly/monthly/yearly reports
- Health check endpoint

## Frontend (React + Tailwind)
- Dashboard
- Statistics (Recharts)
- Goals
- Plans history
- Reports
- Settings (profile + dark/light toggle)
- Zustand state management
